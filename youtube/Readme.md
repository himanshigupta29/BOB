## Satrted to build reading Habit 
- Choose you own book
- Find a book that suits your needs or solves a problems you are looking to solve
- That way you will find the book integresting and will be more motivated to read it
- Choose a time prefreably in the morning
- Tell yourself daily i will read book from 6.30am to 6.45am in this room on this chair
- DO not build your habit of reading book at night before sleep, unleass you are finding just a tool for your nightTime routine and making you doze off!
- Start small, read for short time , or small chunk of page , say 1 page, or 10 mmins
- Repeat that small chunks for 2 months, then grdually increase
- If you don't find a book interesting, change the book
- Do not take pressure why i do not find it integresting enough, just change book
- You will find a book that interests you
